About me

I’m Guillermo Bereche and I’m enrolled in the Interactive Media Design Program in the 1st semester. I was born in Peru and I just got to Canada the last winter. This is my second experience in post secondary studies since I was studying Music in PUCP, which is a university in my country, but I dropped because my family had to move here.

Despite I was studying Music, I was ever interested in the other aspects of Art, especially those related to technology. Although I don’t have a vast experience in design or web development, I liked and was average good in Tech classes and I ever was curious how to combine the virtual world with the art world.

I would like to share a quote that I like, which is:

Hard work betrays none, but dreams betray many. working hard alone doesn't assure you that you'll achieve your dreams. Actually, there are more cases where you don't. Even so, working hard and achieving something is some consolation at least.- Hikigaya Hachiman

Learned Tags:

Here is a list of 5 learned html tags and their description.

Emphasis
<em> </em>. Used to give slightly more important to the text.
Paragraphs
<p> </p>. Used to add paragraphs.
Quotes
<q> </q>. Used to embed small quotations.
Citation
<p> </p>. Used to add a citation for another source, often used with quotations. A person’s name, a URL, a book, a movie title, etc.
Technical Term
<i> </i>. Used to define a technical term of a book title, a thought, sarcasm, another language.
